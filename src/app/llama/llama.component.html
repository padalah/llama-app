Who Are We?

The Llama Store is an online store which sells only llama based products. Our products are made from well-treated llamas domesticated by sustinance farmers in the highlands of Peru. We work with our producers (Peruvian farmers) to promote healthy agricultural practices. Every dollar from your purchase goes to the GreenPeace Organization.
